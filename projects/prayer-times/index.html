Project Overview

prayers is a terminal-based command-line interface tool that shows you today's prayer times in a clean, minimal, and colorful format. You can also specify a custom date to view past or future prayer times.

  • Get daily prayer times instantly in your terminal
  • Specify any date to view prayer times
  • Progress bar showing time left until next prayer
  • Open source and beginner-friendly contributions welcome!

Installation

go install github.com/mabd-dev/prayer-times-cli@latest

Make sure Go is installed and your $GOPATH/bin is in your PATH.
