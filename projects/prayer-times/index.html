Prayer Times Projects

Islamic Prayer Times Tools & Visualizations

A collection of tools and visualizations for Islamic prayer times. Explore an interactive yearly graph showing how prayer times change throughout the year, and use a beautiful terminal-based CLI tool for quick access to daily prayer times.

Project 1

Prayer Times Visualization 2025

An interactive chart showing how prayer times change throughout the year. Observe the beautiful patterns created by the changing seasons and the Earth's tilt.

⬥
Project 2

Prayer Times CLI Tool

A simple, beautiful command-line interface tool that displays today's Islamic prayer times directly in your terminal. Features a clean minimal design with colorful output and progress indicators showing time remaining until the next prayer.

Key Features

⏰

Daily Prayer Times

Get instant access to all five daily prayer times with accurate timings based on your location

📅

Date Flexibility

View prayer times for any date - past, present, or future with simple command flags

📊

Progress Indicator

Visual progress bar showing time remaining until the next prayer

🎨

Colorful Output

Clean, minimal design with color-coded terminal output for better readability

Installation

go install github.com/mabd-dev/prayer-times-cli@latest

Make sure Go is installed and your $GOPATH/bin is in your PATH.
